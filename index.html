What's New?

Accurate classification of apical 2CH and 4CH views is essential for automated cardiac assessment but remains challenging due to acoustic noise, anatomical variability, and limited annotated data.

This study proposes a novel hybrid ensemble framework integrating a lightweight Compact RDN with ResNet34, DenseNet121, EfficientNet-B0, MobileNetV3-Small achieving 96.5% test accuracy and 0.989 AUC on CAMUS dataset.

Key Innovations

  • Ultrasound-optimized Compact RDN
  • Speckle/Gaussian noise augmentations
  • EMA + TTA stabilization
  • 640-dim Hybrid Fusion
